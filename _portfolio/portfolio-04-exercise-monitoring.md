**Tags:** Healthcare AI · Computer Vision

A system leveraging Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to assist users in performing exercises correctly by comparing their execution against reference videos of expert demonstrations. Uses frame-level visual and motion comparison integrated with language-based feedback to generate natural language guidance that helps users improve their form and reduce injury risk. Built for LG Nova.
