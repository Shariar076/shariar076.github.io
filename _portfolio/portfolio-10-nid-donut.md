**Tags:** Document AI · NLP

Fine-tuned the pretrained Donut document transformer model for Key Information Extraction (KIE) on Bangla National ID cards using data generated by [SynthNID](https://aclanthology.org/2023.banglalp-1.13.pdf). Used a mix of real and synthetic data, with the addition of synthetic data yielding significant performance improvements—especially on Bengali-script fields.
